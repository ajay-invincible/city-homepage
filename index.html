Visit Pilibhit

Enjoy culture and ethnicity in this vibrant district close to the Bareilly (U.P.)

Top three activities to do at Pilibhit

Pay a visit to the adventurous forests

The Pilibhit Tiger Reserve is a best place to sight the beautiful forests and fearless tigers.

Go out for the picnic at parks and beaches

The Chuka Beach at Sharda river is a great place to spend a day out with the family and friends.

Experience the Culture and Beauty

The Gauri Shankar Temple is a great place to visit, it has Ardhnarishwar Shivalinga of Lord Shiva
